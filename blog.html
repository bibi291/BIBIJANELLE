Top 5 Summer Accessories

Beadwork in Nigeria is more than decoration — it’s a powerful symbol of culture, history, and identity. From ancient kingdoms like Ife and Benin to modern-day fashion, beads have been used to show royalty, beauty, and spiritual protection. Different ethnic groups — like the Yoruba, Igbo, and Hausa — each have unique styles and meanings for their beads. Traditionally worn by kings, brides, and spiritual leaders, beads still play a big role in Nigerian celebrations today. Now, modern designers are reviving this heritage through handmade bags and jewelry that blend tradition with style. By wearing beads, you're not just accessorizing — you're honoring centuries of African artistry.
